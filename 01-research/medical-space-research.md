# Patient Flow:

## Recommended Circulation
- Entrance → Reception & Registration
- Reception → Waiting Area
- Waiting Area → Consultation Room
- Consultation Room → Treatment Room (if required)
- Treatment Room → Pharmacy / Medication Room (if required)
- Patient exits through Reception
- Staff circulation separated where possible from patient circulation
- Clean and dirty service routes kept separate to reduce infection risk
- Clear signage throughout the facility
- Corridors minimum 1800mm wide for wheelchair and stretcher access
