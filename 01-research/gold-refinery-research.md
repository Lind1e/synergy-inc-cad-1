# Visitor Access Policy:

## Controlled Visitor Entry

### Features:
- No public access permitted
- Entry by management approval only
- Visitors must be pre-authorised before arrival
- Government inspectors, auditors, contractors, maintenance personnel, and approved clients only
- Visitors must present valid identification
- Visitor registration completed before entry
- Temporary visitor access card issued
- Visitors escorted by authorised staff at all times
- Access limited to approved areas only
- No access to refinery floor, vault, assay laboratory, or gold storage unless specifically authorised
- Personal belongings may be searched before entry and exit
- Photography and recording devices prohibited unless authorised
- CCTV monitoring throughout visit
- All visitor movements recorded
