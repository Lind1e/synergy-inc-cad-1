# Service Flow:

## Vehicle Movement Process

### Flow:
- Customer vehicle arrival
- Reception & service booking
- Vehicle inspection
- Assignment to service bay
- Diagnostics (if required)
- Mechanical repair/service
- Parts collection from storage
- Quality inspection
- Vehicle wash/detail (if required)
- Customer collection at reception

### Features:
- One-way vehicle circulation where possible
- Separate customer and workshop traffic
- Efficient workflow with minimal vehicle movement
- Clear communication between reception, parts store, and workshop
