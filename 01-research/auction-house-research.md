# Secure Diamond Storage:

## High-Security Vault
- Size: 5m × 6m (30m²)

### High-Security Storage Cabinets:
- Size: 1200mm (H) × 900mm (W) × 600mm (D)
- Multiple commercial vault cabinets around the perimeter walls
- Cabinets stacked similarly to safety deposit boxes
- Individual labelled lockboxes for each diamond parcel
- Electronic inventory tracking

### Inside Each Cabinet:
- Lockable drawers
- Gem trays
- Individual diamond parcels

### Work Counter:
- Size: 2000mm (L) × 800mm (D) × 900mm (H)

### Position:
- Centre of room
- 1000mm–1200mm clearance around the work counter
- Easy access to all storage cabinets

### Security:
- Separate from viewing rooms
- Reinforced walls
- Grade-rated vault door
- Restricted access (Biometric + PIN + Keycard)

- Motion sensors
- Intruder alarm
- Secure transfer route to viewing rooms
- Backup power supply
- No windows
